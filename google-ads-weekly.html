Optimisations This Week

Google Ads actions taken Jun 5 – Jun 11, 2026.

✓

Brand Search tROAS Raised: 4x to 6x (June 9)

Analysis of May 29–Jun 4 data showed Brand Search delivering a real ROAS of 6.3x against a 4x target — the conservative target was suppressing bids and leaving budget underutilised on high-value searches. Raised to 6x via the API on June 9. Jun 10 response: 7 conversions at 11.7x ROAS. Jun 9 overspent slightly (€145 vs €110 budget cap) — standard day-one behaviour after any bid strategy change. Expected to normalise within 2–3 days.

✓

Gas Bottle tROAS Raised: 1.5x to 3x (June 9)

Rank Lost Impression Share analysis confirmed Gas Bottle was losing 60–72% of available auctions on bid (not budget) — Google was able to spend more but the tROAS target of 1.5x was too conservative to bid competitively. Three weeks of clean conversion data (May 19–Jun 8) confirmed the campaign's real ROAS is consistently above 3x. Raised to 3x via the API on June 9. Jun 9 was a recalibration day (0 conv, €12 spend). Jun 10 delivered the first conversion since the raise. Impression share recovery expected over Jun 12–16.

✓

19 Negative Keywords Added to Hardware Campaign (June 9)

Two batches added following a search terms audit covering May 26–Jun 8. First batch (8 terms): wrong product matches confirmed via site audit — Kenny Fuels sells composite only, not PVC/plastic. Blocked: "pvc fencing", "pvc garden fencing", "pvc decking", "pvc decking boards", "plastic decking", "plastic decking boards" (all phrase match), plus "garden centre" (phrase — intent mismatch, people looking for a physical store to visit) and "what is an acoustic wall panel" (exact, informational). Second batch (11 terms, acoustic panel focus): "what are acoustic", "what is acoustic", "how to fit", "do acoustic panels", "are acoustic panels" (all phrase), "acoustic foam", "b&q", "music studio" (phrase), "pet felt" (exact), "cheap acoustic", "cheapest acoustic" (phrase). Converting terms were left intact: bark mulch wexford (2 conv), topsoil wexford (1 conv), composite fence panels ireland (2 conv).

✓

Hardware Budget Raised: €40 to €70/day (June 11, Ciaran Approved)

Data analysis identified the root cause of Hardware's "Limited by bid strategy" status: at €40/day split across 11 ad groups, average CPC was €0.45 — not competitive enough to win hardware search auctions. Rank Lost IS was 57–90% depending on the ad group (all losing on bid, not budget). Proposal sent to Ciaran on June 11. Ciaran approved the same day. Budget raised to €70/day via the API immediately on approval. Peak season (Jun–Aug) timing — composite decking, acoustic panels, garden products all see peak demand in summer.

Ciaran requested a local push on the Kenergy Solar offering (kennyfuels.ie/kenergy-solar/) — SEAI-backed solar installation through EnergyServ partnership. Campaign approved and planned this week.
