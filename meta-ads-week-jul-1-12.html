Actions This Period

Meta Ads changes made Jul 1–12, 2026.

⚠

Compost: Diagnosed and Fixed a Real Delivery Problem

Margin-adjusted review flagged Compost as a net loss this period. Split the data at the Jul 8 budget scale-up: pre-scale ROAS was 9.03x, post-scale collapsed to 1.45x. Ad-level breakdown showed "Ad 1 Compost 04.26" took 99.7% of the extra spend for 1 purchase, while "Ad 2 Compost Trees" (historically the stronger creative) was nearly starved of delivery. Reverted the ad set to €15/day via API Jul 13.

✎

Attribution Investigation Opened With Graphedia

Confirmed a related Google Ads tracking fix hadn't worked either, prompting a fresh look at the site's tracking setup generally. Three specific checks sent Jul 13: WooCommerce's own Order Attribution settings, whether Complianz blocks that tracking script the same way it blocks Meta's pixel, and a couple of GTM tag coverage gaps. Awaiting response.

✓

CAPI Path Under Evaluation Before Committing Spend

Graphedia proposed a paid CAPI build (2-3 days senior dev work, €995-1,995+VAT, booked for the week of Jul 13). Before sending the access token and starting that work, evaluating a Conversions API Gateway (no-developer alternative) that could avoid most or all of that cost, pending confirmation of who controls the site's Cloudflare DNS access.
