What Changed
April Optimisations

All changes made to the account during April 2026, grouped by priority.

🔧
Critical Fix

Conversion Tracking: Fixed After 4 Months of Inflation

Four purchase conversion actions were simultaneously set as Primary: every real purchase was being counted 4 times, inflating CPL and conversion figures 3-4x and misleading Smart Bidding. Fixed Apr 30 after Admin access was granted. Only "Kenny Fuels - GA4 (web) purchase" (GA4 web) remains Primary. All other purchase, contact, and booking actions set to Secondary. Accurate data collection begins May 2026.

Two broken tags (Contact Us Form, Book appointment GTM) were demoted to Secondary so they have zero impact on bidding. GTM access from Graphedia is still pending to fix the underlying broken tags.

🔧
Critical Fix

Two Broken Bidding Strategies Removed

Brand & General Search had a 6,600% Target ROAS cap: campaign was spending €17/day vs €110 budget. Home Heating Oil Search had a 4,996% Target ROAS cap: near-zero impressions. Both removed and campaigns switched to Maximise Conversion Value (no ROAS constraint). Learning periods underway.

🔧
Critical Fix

Shopping Campaign Paused: Was Spending Budget on Wrong Products

The Shopping campaign was serving ads for fence panels, composite fencing, and decking on fuel searches, spending €302/month at €60.40 CPL. Paused Apr 23. Budget reallocated to Brand & General Search (increased from €100/day to €110/day).

🔧
High Priority

Home Heating Oil Search: Full Keyword Rebuild

Campaign had only "near me" keywords: a search pattern that generates near-zero impressions in Ireland where people search by location name, not "near me." 36 new keywords added Apr 29-30: geographic phrases for Wexford, Wicklow, Carlow, town-level (Gorey, Enniscorthy, Arklow, Ferns), Ireland-wide terms, near-me variants with real volume (heating oil near me 170/mo, home heating oil near me 140/mo, emergency heating oil delivery near me 320/mo), and 7 brand keywords. Impressions jumped from 5-6/day to 70 on Apr 30.

🔧
High Priority

69 Dead Ad Groups Removed from Brand & General Search

The previous agency had created 120+ ad groups using a copy-paste template. 69 enabled ad groups with zero impressions and zero conversions over 30 days were paused across 3 rounds (Apr 27: 8, Apr 28: 46, Apr 29: 15). Before pausing, DataForSEO volumes were checked on all keywords: worthwhile terms were moved to appropriate campaigns (gas canister refills near me, emergency heating oil delivery near me). Categories cleared: hardware, lubricants, firewood, coal, and gas terms duplicated by the Gas Bottle campaign.

🔧
High Priority

Ad Copy Rewritten Across 10 Ad Groups

The previous agency created one ad per ad group across 120+ ad groups: all using the same 3-headline pinned template causing POOR ad strength account-wide. Rewrote 10 priority ad groups with 15 headlines each (H1 pin only), 4 descriptions, product-specific content. Paused skeleton ads. Ad groups fixed: Heating Oil Prices Near Me Today, Heating Oil Suppliers Near Me, Wexford Heating Oil Suppliers, Home Heating Oil Carlow, Heating Oil Delivery Wicklow, Heating Oil For Homes Wicklow, Top Heating Oil Supplier Wicklow, Gas Cylinder For Sale, Kenny Fuels Brand, Fuel Oil For Home Heating. 100+ ad groups remain at Poor: lower priority, to be addressed progressively.

🔧
Optimisation

Broad to Phrase Match: 40+ Keywords Tightened

Broad match keywords across all three campaigns were converted to phrase match to reduce wasted spend on irrelevant queries. Brand & General Search: 15+ keywords converted. Gas Bottle & Cylinders: 17 keywords converted (Superser, butane, propane, forklift cylinder variants). Home Heating Oil Search: 20 near-me keywords converted from broad to phrase, 1 irrelevant keyword paused (home heating propane prices near me).

🔧
Optimisation

Keyword Research: 25+ New Keywords Added Across Campaigns

DataForSEO research (Ireland, location 2372) identified high-volume keywords missing from all three campaigns. Key additions to Brand & General Search: "home heating oil prices ireland" (1,300/mo), "heating oil ireland" (210/mo), "home heating oil ireland" (210/mo), geographic variants for Wexford, Wicklow, Carlow. Key additions to Gas Bottle: "butane gas cylinders" (390/mo), "gas cylinder near me" (320/mo), "gas bottle near me" (260/mo), "lpg gas near me" (260/mo). Competitor negatives added: Calor Gas, Flogas (confirmed: Kenny Fuels does NOT stock Calor; does stock Flo Gas: Flo Gas negative was corrected within the same session).

🔧
Optimisation

Negative Keywords: Wasted Spend Blocked

Multiple negative keyword fixes applied. Removed 5 blocking negatives from Brand & General Search that were excluding high-intent searches: "price", "how much", "rates", "cost", "costs" were blocking "heating oil price" and "how much is home heating oil." Added competitor brand negatives: Flogas, Burkes Oil, TopOil, NaasOil, Certa. Added product exclusions: "oil tank" (Kenny Fuels doesn't sell tanks), "coal merchants" (Gas Bottle campaign). Removed "review" and "reviews" from negative list (were blocking brand reputation searches). Negative keyword audit: reviewed all 200+ negative keywords: removed 2 erroneous blocks, kept legitimate exclusions.
