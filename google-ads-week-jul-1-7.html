Fixable configuration gap, not a permanent limit
  • Unlike Meta, this isn't a legal tracking barrier, it's a missing UTM auto-tagging configuration that was live on the Google Ads side through this whole reporting period.
  • Google's reported conversion value includes Kenergy Solar's synthetic €12,000/conversion bidding value, so order count (66.97 vs 31) is the meaningful comparison here, not revenue.
  • The "Source: Google" bucket isn't confirmed 100% paid either: WooCommerce shows it generically, with no paid/organic qualifier, for any order placed before the fix.
  • A separate 28 orders (€6,352) tagged cleanly as organic and are excluded here entirely, since organic search has nothing to do with Google Ads spend.
  • Fixed Jul 8: a Final URL suffix was added to all campaigns, so orders from Jul 8 onward should tag with an explicit paid source. This ambiguity applies only to data before that date, and should resolve as post-fix orders accumulate over the next few reports.
