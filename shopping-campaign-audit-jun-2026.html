The Strategy Pivot: What Goes on Shopping

Critical
🚫
Heating oil cannot appear on Google Shopping. Google classifies kerosene and diesel as restricted fuels. Any product from those categories submitted to a shopping feed will be immediately rejected. This applies whether the product page is transactional or not. The live price feed development being scoped with Graphedia is not needed for shopping.

💡
How Google Shopping works: Instead of writing an ad, you give Google your product list (name, price, image, link). Google then shows your products as visual tiles at the top of search results when people search for those items. You pay per click only when someone clicks through to your website.

Why this matters for shopping ads: Google Shopping can display star ratings directly on your ads: for example "★★★★★ 4.8 (127 reviews)". Ads with star ratings get significantly more clicks than those without. To unlock this, Kenny Fuels needs at least 100 verified reviews collected through Google Customer Reviews (a free Google programme). We currently have 5 reviews: all 7–8 years old.

What to Do

  • 1️⃣
    Josette registers Kenny Fuels for Google Customer Reviews inside Merchant Center (free, takes 30 minutes). This triggers automatic review request emails to customers after every purchase.
  • 2️⃣
    Ciaran briefs the team to ask customers for a Google review by text or call after every oil delivery and hardware order. A direct review link can be included in delivery confirmation texts.
  • 3️⃣
    Review link: Already in the Graphedia email signature: g.page/r/CTLH5Aw1L13rEBM/review: share this with drivers and staff to send to customers.

Realistic Timeline

  • 📅
    Sept 1: Shopping campaigns launch without stars (still works, just lower CTR initially)
  • 📅
    By end of 2026: If review collection starts now and 8–10 reviews per month are collected, star ratings should appear before Christmas: peak season
  • 📅
    Target: 100 reviews to unlock stars, 150+ for consistent display
💡
This does not block the September 1 launch. It is a parallel workstream that improves performance over time. Start it now so the reviews are building while the campaigns are being set up.
