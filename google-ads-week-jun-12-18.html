Changes Made
Optimisations This Week

Google Ads actions taken Jun 12 – Jun 18, 2026.

✓

Gas Bottle Campaign: Full Restructure (June 15)

A 30-day search terms audit identified significant waste in the Gas Bottle campaign. Over €80 spent in June alone on competitor oil brand terms (Flo Gas, Capital Oil, Vale Oil), heating oil searches, and wrong-category traffic that had no path to conversion. All 22 existing keywords were paused and replaced with three tightly controlled ad groups targeting genuine buying intent: Near Me (12 exact match keywords: "gas bottle near me", "patio gas near me", etc.), Order & Buy (12 exact and phrase keywords: "buy gas bottle ireland", "gas cylinder delivery ireland", etc.), and Product & Geo (17 exact match keywords targeting Wexford, Wicklow, Carlow searchers specifically). 38 negative keywords were added at the same time to block competitor brands, heating oil terms, and own-brand traffic.

✓

Gas Bottle: Display Network Disabled (June 15)

The Gas Bottle campaign had Display Network incorrectly enabled, meaning ads were being served on banner placements across non-search websites. This was not a setting we applied. It was set to FALSE, ensuring all Gas Bottle spend now goes exclusively to Google Search.

✓

Hardware: 3 Zero-Conversion Ad Groups Paused (June 15)

Following a 90-day Hardware campaign analysis, three ad groups were paused that had generated spend with no conversions. Acoustic Wall Panels: 136 clicks, €68 spent, 0 conversions over 90 days. Off-season in June (260 searches/month now vs 1,300 in winter). Will be reactivated in September when seasonal demand returns and once the landing page is updated. Building Materials: 41 clicks, €20 spent, 0 conversions. Search intent is for trade suppliers, not products. Safety Equipment: 16 clicks, €9 spent, 0 conversions. Insufficient search volume (30 searches/month nationwide).

✓

Hardware: 24 Negative Keywords Added and Gardening CPC Raised (June 15)

24 negative keywords added to remove irrelevant traffic: tool brands (Ryobi, Makita, Dewalt, Milwaukee, Parkside), wrong product types (picket fencing, metal fencing, aluminium fencing, steel fencing, wire fencing), outside service area searches (Limerick, Cork, Galway, Mayo, Kerry), and informational terms (decking ideas, fencing ideas, diy fencing). Gardening & Landscaping max CPC raised from €0.01 (placeholder) to €2.00. This is the best performing Hardware ad group at 3.4% CVR and 6.5x ROAS over 90 days. The previous €0.01 CPC was preventing competitive bidding.

✎

Composite Cladding Ad URL Corrected (June 16)

The Composite Cladding ad was pointing to /premium-cladding/, a URL that had been redirected by Graphedia to the correct category page. The ad was updated to point directly to the correct destination, removing the redirect chain. Graphedia had confirmed the server-side redirect on June 15.

●

Kenergy Solar Campaign Launched (June 16)

Four ad groups launched targeting solar installation searches in Wexford, Wicklow, Dublin and Carlow. All keywords on exact match to control intent: Solar Grant & SEAI (capturing SEAI grant and incentive searches), Solar Cost & Price (capturing installation cost and pricing queries), Solar Local Geo (local area searches), and Solar Installer Intent (searches for solar installers and contractors). Budget: €15/day. Bidding: Maximize Conversions with Target CPA of €40 to manage spend during learning. Landing page: kennyfuels.ie/solar-installation/. Full performance review after two weeks.
