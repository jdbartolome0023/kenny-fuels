Kenny FuelsMeta Ads

Gas Cylinders and Home Heating Oil campaign proposal. Prepared by Outposter. Pending Ciaran's approval before any changes are made.

Why Now

Gas cylinders and heating oil are Kenny Fuels' core products and currently have zero paid coverage on Meta. The heating season begins in September and Meta's algorithm needs 2 months of data before it can optimise effectively. Launching July 1 gives us July and August to build that signal so the campaigns are performing at their best when demand peaks in September and October.

Gas cylinders are also a year-round product with strong BBQ season demand through summer, so July is a natural time to launch that campaign too. Both products are already performing well on Google Ads. Adding Meta coverage will extend reach to audiences who aren't actively searching but would respond to the right ad.
