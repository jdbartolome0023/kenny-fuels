What Changed
May Optimisations

All Google Ads, GA4, and GTM actions taken during May 2026, grouped by phase.

Phase 1: May 1–14. Foundation and Cleanup
✅
Critical Fix

Duplicate Conversion Tracking Removed: May 11

Two dead conversion actions (Transactions - All Web Site Data and Purchase GTM) were still set as Primary, sending duplicate signals to Smart Bidding. Set to Secondary. From May 11, Smart Bidding received a clean, single conversion signal for the first time. This was the most impactful fix of the month: without it, Google's algorithm was optimising against inflated and unreliable data.

✅
Critical Fix

Brand Search: tROAS Set to 4x (400%): May 11

Following the tracking fix, Brand Search was switched to Maximize Conversion Value with a tROAS of 400%. The prior 30-day ROAS was 853%, so Google had room to operate while the 400% floor prevents daily overspend. A Smart Bidding learning period began May 14, confirmed ended ~May 18 when Rank Lost IS dropped to under 7%.

🔧
High Priority

Hardware Campaign Launched: Composite Decking: May 5

First hardware campaign built and launched. Four ad groups: General, Price and Buy, Geo, Boards and Planks. 18 phrase match keywords. 15 sitelinks. Budget €25/day on Maximize Clicks. Boards and Planks led with 20%+ CTR on launch. Impression share reached ~65% within the first week. Full 2-week review completed May 21.

🔧
High Priority

68 Non-Converting Ad Groups Paused: May 1–7

A full audit of Brand and General Search identified 68 enabled ad groups with zero conversions over 30 days. All paused to concentrate budget on converting ad groups. Categories cleared: hardware, lubricants, firewood, coal, and gas terms duplicating the Gas Bottle campaign. DataForSEO volumes checked on all keywords before pausing.

🔧
Optimisation

100+ Negative Keywords Added Across All Campaigns: May 15–21

Full search term audit covering May 12–21. Gas Bottle: 55 negatives (competitor brands, wrong product queries). HHO: 13 negatives (competitor brands, wrong-intent terms). Brand Search: 10 negatives (non-fuel terms appearing via broad match). Composite Decking: 23 negatives (competitor brands, installation intent, wrong product).

Phase 2: May 15–21. Tracking Repaired, Account Aligned
✅
Resolved

Consent Mode Live: Complianz Replaced CookieYes: May 15

Complianz replaced CookieYes as the consent management platform on kennyfuels.ie. New GTM workspace configured with ads data redaction enabled and URL passthrough enabled. Both are required for GDPR-compliant consent mode. Website-based conversion tracking (click-to-call, contact form submissions, purchase events) now flows correctly to Google Ads.

✅
Resolved

Conversion Value Rules Applied: May 18

Phone call conversion values were at the €1 Google default since account inception. Value rules applied May 18: HHO phone calls €350 (average heating oil delivery order), Brand Search phone calls €220 (blended fuel order value), Gas Bottle phone calls €90 (average cylinder order). From May 18, ROAS figures reflect real business revenue. All previous conversion value data is near-zero and should not be used as a benchmark.

✅
Resolved

HHO: tCPA Cap Removed, Uncapped Maximize Conversions: May 15

The HHO campaign had a €8 tCPA, set when phone calls were valued at €1. With calls now correctly valued at €350, a €8 CPA was preventing the campaign from entering any auction. Cap removed, switched to Maximize Conversions uncapped. Impression share jumped to 75% and 15 conversions came in within 3 days. Budget formalised at €50/day.

✅
Resolved

Call Recording Restored: Call Extension Fixed: May 20

The call extension pointed to a conversion action that had been removed. Calls were happening through Google's forwarding number but not logging as conversions. Smart Bidding could not see them. Updated to point to the correct active conversion action. All calls from ads record and feed Smart Bidding from May 20 onward.

🔧
Optimisation

Budget Realigned to Full Approved €210/day: May 21

All four campaigns moved to approved daily budgets for the first time. Brand Search increased from €50/day to €110/day (was losing 77% of impression share to budget). Gas Bottle reduced to €15/day (rank-constrained by tROAS target). HHO formalised at €50/day. Hardware increased to €35/day. Total: €210/day as approved by Ciaran.

✅
Completed

Renamed from "KF - Hardware | Composite Decking" to "KF - Hardware." County geo restrictions removed. Hardware products deliver nationwide via courier. A conflicting campaign negative was removed and a disapproved ad URL was corrected. Composite Fencing led impressions (261 in first 3 days). First conversion from Power and Hand Tools on May 27.
