Changes Made
Optimisations This Week

Google Ads and tracking actions taken May 22–28, 2026.

✅

Hardware Campaign Expanded: KF – Hardware (9 Ad Groups, Ireland-Wide)

The campaign was renamed from "KF - Hardware | Composite Decking" to "KF - Hardware" to reflect its expanded scope. County-level geo restrictions (Wexford, Wicklow, Dublin) were removed — fencing, cladding, and other hardware products deliver nationwide via courier, so Ireland-wide targeting now applies. A conflicting campaign-level negative ("composite cladding" phrase match) was removed as it had been blocking cladding searches from the new Composite Cladding ad group. Nine new ad groups launched: Composite Fencing (18 keywords), Composite Cladding (11 keywords), Gardening and Landscaping (12 keywords), Building Materials (16 keywords), Power and Hand Tools (17 keywords), Acoustic Wall Panels (8 keywords), Welding Supplies (7 keywords), Safety Equipment (9 keywords), and Animal Feed (7 keywords). Composite Fencing led impressions in the first 3 days (261 impressions). First conversion from Power and Hand Tools recorded May 27.

🔧

Budget Swap: Gas Bottle Restored to €35/day, Hardware Reduced to €15/day

Gas Bottle was showing "Limited by budget" at €15/day — it had been hitting its tROAS 1.5x target and wanting more spend. Budget restored to the original approved €35/day. Hardware was reduced from €35/day to €15/day: the original Composite Decking-only campaign was only spending ~€29/day due to limited search volume for a niche product in a small geographic market. The newly restructured campaign now has 9 ad groups with significantly more combined volume — budget will be reviewed for an increase after 2 weeks of data (June 9). Total daily spend remains at €210/day as approved.

✅

PixelYourSite Tracking Issue Fully Resolved

PixelYourSite had been injecting a duplicate GA4 tracking script on kennyfuels.ie since before we took over the account. This caused GA4 sessions and events to be inflated, making engagement metrics unreliable. The plugin was deleted and the script was removed from theme files on May 27–28. GA4 real-time confirmed clean: the "Pageview" (capital P, PixelYourSite signature) event is no longer firing. The ads_conversion_Checkout_1 event (also PixelYourSite) was removed as part of the same fix. GA4 data from May 28 onwards is clean and single-source through GTM.

✅

Composite Fencing Ad URL Fixed: Ad Approved

The Composite Fencing ad was disapproved by Google Ads with a "Destination not working" policy violation. The final URL had been set to /fence-kit/ (a flat URL that returns a 404). The correct URL is /product-category/hardware/fence-kit/. The URL was corrected via the API and the ad was re-submitted for review. Ad confirmed approved.
