Changes Made
Optimisations This Week

Google Ads and GA4 account actions taken May 1–8, 2026.

🔧

Conflicting Negatives Removed and General Negatives Applied

A full negative keyword audit across Brand and General Search identified broad match negatives that were blocking active ad groups from serving. Three were removed: "price" BROAD (blocking Heating Oil Prices Near Me Today, Best Home Heating Oil Prices Wexford, and Oil Prices Wexford), "prices" BROAD (same groups), and "cheapest" BROAD (blocking Cheapest Oil Prices Wexford). The competitor negative "top oil" BROAD was changed to EXACT so the competitor brand remains blocked while the Top Heating Oil Supplier Wicklow ad group is unblocked.

The General Negatives shared list was found missing from all three KF Fuel campaigns. This list blocks non-commercial queries such as photos, discount code searches, YouTube searches, and job-seeker terms. Applied to Brand and General Search, Home Heating Oil Search, and Gas Bottle and Cylinders. The firewood BROAD negative in the Fuel Campaigns - Hardware Exclusions list was also removed — Kenny Fuels sells firewood, so it was incorrectly blocking all firewood ad groups.

🔧

Ad Group-Level Negatives Added

Search term audits identified two categories of irrelevant traffic passing through after the General Negatives list was applied.

Kenny Fuels brand ad group (9 phrase negatives): kevin building supplies, a142 mesh, discount code, petrol station, service station, building materials, decorative stone, landscaping, gravel.

Fuel Oil For Home Heating (10 competitor phrase negatives): o neills fuels, burkes oil, klass oil, capitol oil, corrib oil, emo oil, certas, texoil, atmos boilers, bunded tank. Competitor fuel brand searches will not convert for Kenny Fuels.

Campaign-level, Brand and General (6 negatives added May 5): kevin, zf, 20w50, boilers (broad); kiln dried shavings, wicklow wood fuels (phrase). Added following a May 1–4 search term review.

⏸

Gas Cylinder For Sale Ad Group Paused

The Gas Cylinder For Sale ad group in Brand and General Search was duplicating the dedicated Gas Bottle and Cylinders campaign. In May 1–3 it spent €23.62 with 0 conversions while the Gas Bottle campaign recorded 2 conversions over the same period. Pausing this ad group consolidates gas cylinder budget into the campaign that is actually converting and removes an internal auction conflict.

🚀

Composite Decking Campaign Launched

The first hardware campaign — KF Hardware | Composite Decking — was built and launched on May 5.

Setup: Budget €15/day (increased to €25/day May 7), Maximise Clicks bidding, targeting Wexford, Wicklow, and Carlow. Hardware Campaigns - Fuel Exclusions negative list applied.

4 ad groups: General (4 keywords), Price and Buy (5 keywords), Geo (4 keywords), Boards and Planks (5 keywords). Each has 15 headlines and 4 descriptions. No keyword overlap between groups. 15 campaign-level sitelinks added covering all hardware product categories.

Early performance (May 5–7): 202 impressions, 41 clicks, 20.3% CTR, €1.36 avg CPC, €56 spend. Boards and Planks leading volume. CPCs in the lower end of the expected range for composite decking in Ireland.

✅

GA4 Conversion Tracking Cleaned Up

A full audit of GA4 key events identified four incorrectly configured events inflating conversion counts throughout April. All removed.

Removed May 5: view_item (product page views were counting as conversions), Purchase_2 (broken duplicate tag, 84 fires with €0 revenue), ads_conversion_Checkout_1 (duplicate of begin_checkout, 229 fires at €0), ads_conversion_Shopping_Cart_1 (duplicate of add_to_cart, 32 fires at €0).

Removed May 8: begin_checkout and add_to_cart unmarked as GA4 key events. Only purchase remains as a key event — giving a clean picture of actual buyer intent. Google Ads conversion actions reviewed: only two remain as Primary (Calls from ads and GA4 web purchase). All others are Secondary.

🔧

Dual GA4 Tag Root Cause Identified

A full investigation identified why Brand and General shows 0 conversions in Google Ads while GA4 still shows purchase revenue. Two GA4 measurement IDs are firing on every page: G-0RM5FH1MST (PixelYourSite plugin, capturing ~89% of sessions) and G-BMD8SXLKC0 (direct gtag.js script). The property linked to Google Ads is only capturing 11% of sessions — so 89% of purchase events are invisible to Google Ads.

A consolidated fix request was sent to the client on May 5 for forwarding to Graphedia. Required actions: (1) confirm which measurement ID belongs to property 396621665 and remove the duplicate; (2) fix Contact Us Form and Book Appointment conversion tags in GTM; (3) install Google's phone snippet for website call tracking; (4) add form ID differentiation so Contact, Booking, and Quote forms are tracked separately.

🔧

Brand and General Search: Bidding Strategy Updated

Switched from a portfolio Maximize Conversion Value strategy to standard campaign-level Maximize Conversion Value. The portfolio strategy was blocking the ability to set a maximum CPC bid limit. The algorithm now optimises on this campaign's data only. Average CPCs trended down from €0.85 to €0.52 over the week — the negative keyword fixes from May 4 are reducing high-CPC irrelevant queries. The €50/day daily budget acts as the practical spend ceiling.
