Optimisations This Week

Google Ads actions taken May 29 – Jun 4, 2026.

✅

HHO Sunday Removed from Ad Schedule

0 conversions were confirmed on both monitored Sundays (May 24 and May 31) despite spending €18–32 each day. Sunday has been removed from the HHO ad schedule via the API. HHO now runs Monday to Saturday only (9am–5pm weekdays, 9am–3pm Saturday). This removes approximately €128/month in wasted Sunday spend and allows that budget to work harder on higher-converting weekdays.

✅

Animal Feed Ad Group Paused

Kenny Fuels no longer stocks animal feed products. The Animal Feed ad group has been paused via the API to prevent ads serving for products that are not available. The ad group remains in the account and can be reactivated if the product range is reintroduced.

🔧

Deep Account Analysis: Gas Bottle and Brand Search Identified for June 9 Action

A full data analysis across May 19 – June 4 identified two priority optimisations for June 9. Gas Bottle: Rank Lost IS at 60–72% confirms tROAS 1.5x is blocking the campaign from bidding competitively. Raising to 3–4x should significantly increase impression share and conversions. Brand Search: Budget Lost IS consistently 45–60% shows the campaign is hitting its €110/day cap regularly despite strong quality metrics (Rank Lost IS under 10%). Raising tROAS from 4x to 6–7x at June 9 will help Google use the budget more efficiently on the highest-value searches.

Upcoming Priorities

Performance-driven priorities for the coming weeks.

June 9: Gas Bottle tROAS

Raise Gas Bottle tROAS: 1.5x to 3–4x

This is the single biggest opportunity in the account right now. Gas Bottle is losing 60–72% of available auctions because its tROAS of 1.5x is too conservative. Real ROAS over the clean data window (May 19 – June 4) is consistently above 3x. Raising to 3–4x should significantly increase impression share from ~15% toward 30%+, unlocking conversions that are currently being passed over.

June 9: Brand Search tROAS

Raise Brand Search tROAS: 4x to 6–7x

Brand Search is budget-constrained (Budget Lost IS 45–60%) not bid-constrained (Rank Lost IS under 10%). Raising the tROAS target from 4x to 6–7x will allow Google to focus the existing €110/day budget on the highest-value searches rather than spreading it across all branded queries. Real ROAS has been consistently 9x+ since tracking was fixed.
