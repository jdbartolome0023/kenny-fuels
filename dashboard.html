Ad Copy Rewritten — 7 Priority Ad Groups (Brand and General Search)

All priority heating oil ad groups in Brand and General Search had Poor ad strength — the lowest possible rating. The previous agency created every ad using a 3-pinned-headline template with identical generic copy ("Affordable, Reliable Service", "Most Trusted Ireland Supplier") across 120+ ad groups. The following 7 ad groups were each rewritten with 15 unique headlines, H1 pinned to the target keyword, H2-H15 unpinned, and 4 descriptions with D1 pinned only. The old Poor ads were paused in each.

Ad groups rewritten: Heating Oil Prices Near Me Today, Heating Oil Suppliers Near Me, Wexford Heating Oil Suppliers, Home Heating Oil Carlow, Home Heating Oil Company Carlow, Heating Oil Delivery Wicklow, Heating Oil For Homes Wicklow.

Headlines use real USPs sourced from the Kenny Fuels website: EU Business Award Winner 2021, Fully Trackable Delivery, Best Budget Heating Oil, Save With Online Pricing, Emergency Heating Oil Delivery.

Best Home Heating Oil Prices Wexford was already rated Excellent — no changes made. Gas Bottle and Cylinders (Excellent) and Home Heating Oil Search (Excellent with 4 weak headlines replaced) also reviewed.

Flogas (separate from Flo Gas) is a direct competitor fuel brand. A broad match negative keyword for "flogas" was added at campaign level to prevent Kenny Fuels ads from showing on Flogas brand searches.

🔧

Brand Keyword Performance Drop Investigated

The "kenny fuels" brand keyword showed clicks down 24% and conversions down 43% vs the prior period. Investigation confirmed this is a search volume decline, not a competitor issue. The drop is a reduction in total branded searches, not a Google Ads problem. Recommendation: brand awareness activity off-platform to rebuild search demand.
