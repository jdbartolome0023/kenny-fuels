Changes Made
Optimisations This Week

Google Ads and GTM actions taken May 7–14, 2026.

🔧

Bidding Guardrails Applied Across All Campaigns

All four active campaigns now have bidding constraints that cap waste spend while keeping Smart Bidding in control.

Brand Search: Target ROAS set to 400% on Maximize Conversion Value. 30-day ROAS is well above this, giving Google room to operate while preventing uncapped overspend.

Gas Bottle and Cylinders: Target ROAS set to 150%. Campaign had no guardrail — 30-day ROAS of 190% means the 150% target gives Google room while adding a spending brake.

Home Heating Oil Search: Switched to Maximize Conversions with a target CPA of €8. The low tCPA makes Google selective about which auctions it enters, naturally capping daily spend without a hard budget cap.

🔧

Ad Scheduling and Device Targeting Overhauled

A conversion timing analysis across all campaigns (Apr 25–May 11) confirmed every conversion happened during business hours. Overnight and weekend spend was generating zero conversions.

Ad schedules set: Brand Search 6am–midnight, Gas Bottle 7am–6pm, Home Heating Oil 6am–6pm, Composite Decking 6am–10pm. Saves approximately €18/day with no conversions lost.

Device adjustments: Desktop −70%, Tablet −100% across all campaigns. Every conversion in the analysis period was on mobile. Desktop was spending €24.67/day at double the CPC with zero conversions. Saves approximately €19/day, zero conversions lost.

✅

Conversion Tracking Rebuilt

A full audit of conversion actions identified that phone calls were not visible to Smart Bidding. Four actions reconfigured as primary, each assigned a €220 blended value per lead (weighted average across HHO oil orders at €350, gas bottle orders at €90, and brand/general enquiries at €220).

Actions now primary: Phone Number Call (website), Click to call (website), Calls from ads (Google native), Contact Us Form submission. Google Ads can now see and value phone call activity across all lead types.

Outdated actions demoted: Two old Universal Analytics conversion actions removed from primary — they stopped recording data when UA was deprecated but were still counting as primary conversions and distorting Smart Bidding signals.

✅

GTM Conversion Tag Labels Fixed

Two GTM tags were firing against removed Google Ads conversion actions, meaning the events were being sent but never recorded. Both fixed and verified working in GTM preview.

Contact Us Form tag: Corrected to fire against the active "Contact Us Form" conversion action. The tag was previously named "Book Appointment" — there is only one contact form on the site (on /contact-us/), no separate booking form.

Phone click tag: Corrected to fire against the active "Click to call GTM" conversion action. These events will become visible once the CookieYes consent issue is resolved.

🔧

Brand Search: Phone Calls Added as Bidding Goal

An audit of Brand Search campaign settings found that phone call leads were excluded from the campaign's conversion goals — only purchases were set as biddable. With website purchases blocked by the CookieYes consent issue, Smart Bidding had no signal to work with and was effectively optimising toward zero. Phone call leads are now included as a biddable goal alongside purchases. Smart Bidding will enter a 1–2 week learning period as it recalibrates toward the call signal.

🔧

Brand Search Restructured and Renamed

The campaign was renamed from "Brand and General Search" to "KF – Fuel | Brand Search" to reflect its current scope. Non-brand ad groups (firewood, logs, coal, gas, LPG, lubricants, hardware, solid fuel) were paused — these keywords belong in dedicated campaigns and were competing internally with the brand ad group. Only the Kenny Fuels brand ad group remains active, which carries a Quality Score of 10.

🔧

Keyword Expansion: Gas Bottle and Home Heating Oil

Gas Bottle and Cylinders — 13 phrase match keywords added: gas cylinder, gas bottles, butane gas, camping gas, lpg gas, patio gas, propane gas, gas canister, patio gas cylinder, lpg cylinder, gas refill, gas suppliers near me, gas provider near me. These expand reach beyond the original BBQ-focused terms into year-round cylinder buying intent.

Home Heating Oil Search — 8 phrase match keywords added: heating oil, home heating oil, kerosene, oil delivery, and near-me variants. Low-quality-score and zero-click keywords paused in both campaigns to concentrate budget on active performers.

🔧

Negative Keywords Expanded

Account-level: "surelite" added — coal brand, Kenny Fuels does not sell coal (€7.60 wasted over 17 days).

Home Heating Oil Search: Competitor negatives added — certa oil, newline oil. Certa Oil alone had wasted €21.64 over 17 days with zero conversions. Brand negative "kenny" added at campaign level to prevent brand terms competing with Brand Search.

Gas Bottle and Cylinders: Flogas block reinforced with phrase and exact match — previous broad block was insufficient. Competitor negatives added: glen fuels, campus oil, naas oil, morris oil, burke oil. Brand negative "kenny" added at campaign level.
