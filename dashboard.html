Changes Made
Optimisations This Week

Google Ads and GTM actions taken May 15–21, 2026.

✅

Home Heating Oil: Spend Cap Removed

The HHO campaign had a target CPA cap of €8 in place — a spend brake that had been set when phone calls were valued at a €1 placeholder. With phone calls now correctly valued at €350 each, a €8 tCPA was preventing the campaign from entering any auction. The cap was removed and the campaign switched to Maximize Conversions (uncapped). Impression share jumped to 75% and 15 calls came in within 3 days. Budget formalised at €50/day to reflect actual spend. A tROAS assessment is scheduled for late June once 5 weeks of clean data are available.

✅

Consent Mode Live: Complianz Active

Complianz has replaced CookieYes as the active consent management platform on kennyfuels.ie. A new GTM workspace was created with the correct configuration — ads data redaction enabled and URL passthrough enabled, both required for GDPR-compliant consent mode. The banner is confirmed live on the website. Website-based conversion tracking (click-to-call, contact form, purchase events) is now flowing correctly to Google Ads for the first time since the CookieYes issue began.

🔧

Budget Reallocated to Full Approved €210/day

All four campaigns have been moved to their approved daily budgets for the first time. Brand Search increased from €50/day to €110/day — it was the most budget-constrained campaign, losing 77% of impression share to budget on May 20. Gas Bottle reduced from €50/day to €15/day as it is rank-constrained by its bid target and additional budget was not generating more calls. HHO increased from €25/day to €50/day to match actual spend. Composite Decking increased from €25/day to €35/day for the hardware season. Total remains at €210/day as approved.

🔧

Landing Page Targeting Updated: Gas Bottle and HHO

Both campaigns were directing all ad traffic to the homepage. Updated to send users to the correct destination pages. Gas Bottle ads now point to the gas product category page (kennyfuels.ie/product-category/gas/), which shows all Flogas products with pricing. HHO ads now point to the dedicated heating oil page (kennyfuels.ie/home-heating-oil-wexford-carlow-wicklow/). Quality scores on price-intent keywords are expected to improve over the next 1–2 weeks as Google registers the updated landing page experience.

✅

Call Recording Restored Across All Campaigns

The call extension attached to all campaigns was pointing to a conversion action that had been removed. Calls were happening through the Google forwarding number but not being logged as conversions, meaning Smart Bidding could not see or optimise toward them. The call extension has been updated to point to the correct active conversion action. All calls from ads are now recording and feeding Smart Bidding from May 20 onward.

🔧

Home Heating Oil: Search-Only Mode Enabled

The HHO campaign had Display Network enabled, meaning ads were showing on third-party websites and apps in addition to Google Search. Search campaigns should only show on Google Search — Display placements have very different intent profiles and conversion rates. Display Network has been disabled. HHO is now Search-only.

🔧

100+ Negative Keywords Added Across All Campaigns

A full search term audit covering May 12–21 identified irrelevant, competitor, and wrong-intent queries spending budget without converting. Over 100 campaign-level negative keywords were added across all four campaigns.

Gas Bottle (55 negatives): Competitor brands (Burke Oil, Capital Oil, Calor, Glen Fuels, Klass Oil, etc.), wrong product queries (oil tanks, oil delivery, bunded oil tank), and completely irrelevant terms (postcrete, air compressor, beer gas regulator, replace gas boiler).

HHO (13 negatives): Competitor brands (Glen Fuels, Campus Oil, Bates Oil, Whelan Oil, Atlantic Oils, etc.) and wrong-intent terms (oil, top oil, vale oil, jones oil).

Brand Search (10 negatives): Non-fuel terms appearing due to broad match (garden stone, tonne bags, hardwood timber, bulk compost).

Composite Decking (23 negatives): Competitor brands (Done Deal, Chadwicks, Screwfix, Millboard, Whiteriver, Envirobuild, etc.), installation intent (decking installers, how to install, laying composite decking), and wrong product (composite cladding, recycled plastic planks).

✅

Phone Number Click Tracking Added via GTM

A second phone call conversion action — Phone Number Call (for clicks on the tel: number displayed on the website) — now has a GTM tag in place and is live. This action has been set as secondary (data only) to avoid double-counting with the primary Click to Call conversion action. All tel: link clicks on kennyfuels.ie now record to both actions, giving a complete picture of phone activity across ad-driven and organic traffic.

Upcoming Priorities

Performance-driven priorities for the coming weeks.

Awaiting Graphedia

HHO Landing Page: Pricing Information

The Home Heating Oil page currently shows only a quote request form with no visible pricing. Google flags this as a relevance gap on price-intent searches ("heating oil prices Wexford" etc.), which lowers Quality Scores and raises CPCs. Adding indicative price ranges by county and volume would improve QS and lower the cost per lead. Request sent to Ciaran to pass to Graphedia.

Awaiting Ciaran

Composite Decking: Delivery Zone and Page Content

Two clarifications requested from Ciaran: confirmation of which counties are covered for standard delivery (the product page lists two slightly different county sets), and direction on adding a sample project cost to the category page. Once delivery zones are confirmed, ad targeting will be tightened to match. Product reviews have also been requested to build buyer trust for the new category.

Awaiting Ciaran

Duplicate GA4 Tracking Removal

A second GA4 tag is firing on kennyfuels.ie outside of GTM, installed by a previous agency. This causes some GA4 events to be counted twice. Ciaran has been asked to contact the agency to disable the integration. Once removed, all GA4 data will be clean and single-source through GTM.

Monitor — May 28

Brand Search Learning Period Check

The Brand Search conversion goal was corrected on May 14. Smart Bidding typically takes 2 weeks to recalibrate following a goal change. From May 28, impression share and CPC should be stabilising. The brand budget is now at the approved €110/day level — if IS remains below 60% after the learning period, a tROAS adjustment will be considered.

Gas Bottle tROAS Review

The current tROAS of 1.5x was set as a conservative brake when tracking was unreliable. With 3 weeks of clean data available from early June (post-Complianz and post-conversion value rules), the real ROAS will be clearer. If it consistently sits above 3x, the tROAS target will be raised to unlock more impression share for Gas Bottle.

Review — Late June

HHO and Full Account tROAS Baseline

HHO is running Maximize Conversions uncapped while Smart Bidding learns the new call values. A tROAS for HHO will be considered in late June after 5–6 weeks of clean data. A full account tROAS baseline review across all campaigns is also planned for mid-June — the first point at which Complianz, correct conversion values, and repaired call tracking will all have been live for 3+ weeks simultaneously.
