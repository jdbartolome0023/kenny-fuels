Changes Made
Optimisations This Week

Google Ads account actions taken during this reporting period.

✓

Shopping Campaign Paused Serving Wrong Products

The Shopping campaign was showing ads for fence panels, composite fencing, and decking boards not fuel products. It spent €71.25 this week with zero fuel leads. The root cause is an incorrect product feed. The campaign has been paused immediately to stop the waste. It will only be reactivated once the product feed is corrected to include fuel products only.

✓

Brand Cannibalization Fixed Gas Bottle & Cylinders

The Gas Bottle & Cylinders campaign was bidding on brand terms like "kenny fuels" and "kenny fuels ferns" at €0.68 CPC the same clicks the Brand & General Search campaign captures at €0.11. This 6x cost inflation was the primary cause of the €51.88 CPL this week. We added 14 negative keywords (exact match brand terms + irrelevant product terms) to prevent this. The impact will show in next week's CPL.

✓

Target ROAS Cap Removed Brand & General Search

The Brand & General Search campaign had a Target ROAS of 6,600% set meaning Google would only enter auctions where it could generate €66 return for every €1 spent. This was causing the campaign to spend only €17/day against a €100 budget. The ROAS target has been removed and the campaign now runs on Maximise Conversion Value without a constraint. The campaign is in a learning period (approximately 1 week) and spend should normalise to the full €110/day budget shortly.

✓

12 Zero-Conversion Ad Groups Paused Brand & General Search

12 ad groups within the Brand & General Search campaign had spent a combined €78 over 30 days with zero conversions. These included Home Heating Oil Delivery (€18.88), Bags Of Firewood For Sale (€12.68), Heating And Oil Companies (€9.14), Coal Order Online (€8.61), and others. All have been paused. Budget is now concentrated in the ad groups that actually convert Kenny Fuels brand, Fuel Oil For Home Heating, and the Carlow/Wexford/Wicklow local groups.

✓

Blocking Negative Keywords Removed Brand & General Search

Five keywords were incorrectly listed as negative keywords inside the Brand & General Search campaign: "price", "how much", "rates", "cost", and "costs". These were blocking high-intent searches like "heating oil price today", "how much is home heating oil", and "heating oil rates near me". All five have been removed, allowing the campaign to show for these valuable searches.

✓

Account Structure Cleaned Up Pre-Hardware Campaign Launch

All 4 active campaigns have been renamed to a consistent format (KF - [Category] | [Product]). 16 hardware-themed ad groups were removed from the fuel search campaign where they didn't belong. Display Network was turned off on the Brand & General Search campaign (it should only run on Google Search). Two shared negative keyword lists were created: "Hardware Campaigns - Fuel Exclusions" and "Fuel Campaigns - Hardware Exclusions" the latter has been applied to all 3 active fuel campaigns to prevent cross-contamination when hardware campaigns launch.

✓

Conversion Tracking Fix Scheduled Thursday 24 April

The account currently has 4 purchase conversion actions all set to "Primary" and firing simultaneously meaning every sale is being counted 4 times. This inflates all lead numbers and CPL figures by 3–4x, causing Google's Smart Bidding to make decisions based on incorrect data. We have requested temporary Admin access from the client to fix this on Thursday 24 April. The fix involves keeping only one Primary purchase conversion and setting the other three to Secondary. Once completed, all reported metrics will reflect true performance.
