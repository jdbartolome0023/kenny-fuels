Actions This Week

June 1 - June 5, 2026.

✅

3-Month Audit Completed (June 1)

Full performance review covering March, April, and May 2026. Account-level, campaign-level, ad set, and creative breakdown. 3-month ROAS of 118x on €3,944 spend generating €465,786 in revenue. Key finding: off-season hit hard in May with spend doubling while purchases fell 61%. Full report: View audit report

✅

Pixel Setup Completed (June 1-4)

Partner access confirmed in Events Manager. Advanced matching enabled with all 8 parameters: email, phone, name, gender, location, country, date of birth, external ID. Custom events: Ciaran confirmed 11 events and blocked __missing_event. Graphedia request raised June 4 and followed up June 5 for fbq('init') advanced matching code fix on kennyfuels.ie.

✅

June Strategy Approved by Ciaran (June 3)

Strategy submitted June 1 covering budget reallocation, June KPIs, September heating season prep, and audience expansion. Ciaran confirmed: "That looks perfect. We can scale back on ad spend for summer and ramp up in winter."

🔧

All Budget Changes Implemented (June 5)

Firewood B2C and B2B reduced from €15 to €5/day each to preserve algorithm learnings in off-season. Compost scaled from €15 to €30/day. Sand+Stones scaled from €15 to €25/day. Total account budget increased from €60 to €85/day. All confirmed via Meta API.

🚀

BOF Retargeting Reactivated (June 5)

BOF_Retargeting campaign reactivated at €20/day. Compost Trees ad (95.9x ROAS) duplicated into the New Sales Ad Set. Targeting: Web Visits Last 30 Days, excludes recent purchasers, all Ireland. Campaign confirmed active via API (Ad ID: 120244716645220643). This is the first retargeting running in the account since November 2024.

🔧

Compost Creative Split Fixed (June 5)

Ad 1 Compost 04.26 paused (confirmed via API: PAUSED). Compost Trees ad (95.9x ROAS, €4.99 CPA) now receives the full €30/day budget. Previously, the inferior ad (49.6x ROAS, €5.81 CPA) was receiving 7x more budget despite lower performance.

🔧

Geography Expanded: Compost and Sand+Stones (June 5)

Both Compost and Sand+Stones ad sets expanded from 5 counties to 8 counties. Kildare, Kilkenny, and Tipperary added alongside the original Carlow, Dublin, Waterford, Wexford, and Wicklow. Rationale: account frequency was 7.14, audience pool was saturating. Larger reach pool reduces frequency and extends ad exposure to new potential customers.

🚀

Gas and Heating Oil Campaign Brief Submitted (June 5)

Full campaign brief prepared and submitted to Ciaran for approval. Two new campaigns proposed: Gas Cylinders (€30/day) and Home Heating Oil (€30/day), both launching July 1. Must be live 2 months before September heating season to build algorithm signal. Brief: View campaign brief
