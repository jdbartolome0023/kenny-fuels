What Changed
June Optimisations

All Google Ads, GA4, and GTM actions taken during June 2026.

Phase 1: Jun 1–14. Bid Strategy Raises and Cleanup
✅
Optimisation

HHO Sunday Removed

HHO recorded 0 conversions on Sunday May 24 (€18 spend) and Sunday May 31 (€18 spend). Pattern confirmed — Sunday is not a converting day for heating oil. Day-parting updated Jun 3: campaign now runs Mon–Sat only.

✅
Optimisation

Brand Search tROAS Raised 4x to 6x

Actual Brand Search ROAS confirmed at 7.3x — well above the 4x target. The old target was capping impression share unnecessarily. tROAS raised to 6x on Jun 9. IS improved and rank-lost IS dropped following the change. The "kenny fuels" keyword leads: €0.21 CPC, 45.9% CTR, QS 10.

✅
Optimisation

Gas Bottle tROAS Raised 1.5x to 3x

Real Gas Bottle ROAS tracking 3.4x. Old 1.5x tROAS was throttling IS to ~20%. Raised to 3x Jun 9. This was later relaxed (see Phase 3) after the Jun 15 restructure reset the learning period.

Phase 2: Jun 15–21. Restructure, Solar Launch, Consent Confirmed
✅
High Priority

Gas Bottle Full Restructure

All 22 old keywords paused. 3 focused ad groups built: Near Me (local intent), Order & Buy (purchase intent), Product & Geo (branded product + county targeting). 41 phrase/exact keywords. Display Network disabled. 38 negatives added: competitor oil brands, heating oil terms, gas accessories, helpline intent, price comparison sites. Short-term volume drop during learning period — expected.

✅
Resolved

Consent Mode Confirmed Working

Click to Call GTM conversion action recording 149 primary conversions in 30 days — confirming CookieYes consent signals are correctly passing to Google Ads. All conversion actions (calls from ads, click-to-call GTM, GA4 purchases) confirmed Primary and correctly valued.
