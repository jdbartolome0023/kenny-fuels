Changes Made
Actions This Week

June 5 - June 11, 2026.

🔧

Geography Corrected: Compost and Sand+Stones (June 9)

Ciaran confirmed Kildare, Kilkenny, and Tipperary are outside Kenny Fuels' current service area. Both Compost and Sand+Stones ad sets reverted from 8 counties to the correct 5: Carlow, Dublin, Waterford, Wexford, Wicklow. Updated via API.

🔧

BOF Retargeting Audience Expanded (June 9)

Frequency hit 9.17 across 5 days on a pool of 2,295 people — well above the healthy 3-5x range. Created a new Web Visits Last 60 Days audience (ID: 120245054721650643) and swapped it into the BOF ad set. By June 11 frequency had dropped to 2.51. Issue fully resolved.

✅

Gas and Heating Oil Budget Approved by Ciaran (June 9)

Ciaran replied to the campaign brief submitted June 5: "Happy with the budget." Additional €60/day confirmed for the two new campaigns. Campaign structures planned for June 16-20. Launch target: July 1, 2026.

⚠️

Compost: Second Creative Requested (June 11)

Compost had 11 purchases in the first four days of the week (June 5-8), then zero purchases on June 9, 10, and 11. This pattern suggests the Compost Trees ad is beginning to fatigue after running as the sole creative. A second image or short video has been requested from Ciaran to give the algorithm options and restore momentum.

⚠️

Pixel Purchase Value Issue Identified (June 11-12)

Meta Events Manager diagnostics flagged that 23% of Purchase events are firing without a value field. The pixel code is triggering the purchase event on some orders but not passing the order amount through. This affects ad optimisation and ROAS reporting accuracy. Graphedia have been contacted to identify and fix the relevant plugin or code handling the pixel on kennyfuels.ie.

⚠️

Heating Oil Landing Page Pricing (June 11)

Ciaran flagged that the heating oil landing page (kennyfuels.ie/home-heating-oil-wexford-carlow-wicklow/) has no pricing visible to users. This is a blocker for the Gas and Heating Oil campaign launch on July 1. Graphedia have been contacted to add pricing before launch.
