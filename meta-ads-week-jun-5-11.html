This Week's Notes
  • 21 purchases, €6,849 revenue, 11.0x ROAS on €623 spend. Significant improvement on Week 1 (Jun 1–4: 6 purchases, €1,140 revenue, 6.5x ROAS on €176 spend), driven by the full June budget structure being live for the first time. Compost and Sand+Stones were the two main converters. Firewood B2C had 0 purchases but recorded 3 ATCs and 3 initiate checkouts worth €871 in cart — off-season browsing but not converting, which is expected at this time of year.
  • Compost delivered all 11 purchases in the first four days (Jun 5–8), then zero for three consecutive days. This pattern points to creative fatigue — the Compost Trees ad has been the sole active creative since Jun 5 when the inferior ad was paused. A second image or short video has been requested from Ciaran to give the algorithm options and restore consistent delivery. The ad itself is proven (95.9x ROAS over 3 months) so this is a creative supply issue, not a targeting or bidding problem.
  • BOF Retargeting frequency hit 9.17 after 5 days on the original 30-day audience of 2,295 people. Standard retargeting cap is 3–5x per week. On June 9 the audience was expanded to Web Visits Last 60 Days, doubling the pool size. By June 11 frequency had dropped to 2.51. Issue resolved. The campaign delivered 2 purchases (€861.90) for the week but the high frequency in the first half was suppressing efficiency.
  • Gas and Heating Oil campaign budget approved by Ciaran on June 9 (€60/day additional). Campaign structures will be built June 16–20 for a July 1 launch. A blocker remains on the heating oil landing page: no pricing is currently visible to users on kennyfuels.ie/home-heating-oil-wexford-carlow-wicklow/. Graphedia have been contacted. Also flagged this week: Meta Events Manager diagnostics show 23% of Purchase events are missing the value field. Graphedia have been asked to identify and fix the relevant plugin.

Changes Made
Optimisations This Week

Meta Ads actions taken Jun 5 – Jun 11, 2026.

✓

Geography Corrected: Compost and Sand+Stones (June 9)

Ciaran confirmed on June 9 that Kildare, Kilkenny, and Tipperary are outside Kenny Fuels' current service area. Both Compost and Sand+Stones ad sets were reverted from 8 counties to the correct 5: Carlow, Dublin, Waterford, Wexford, Wicklow. Updated via the Meta API. The initial expansion to 8 counties was applied June 5 to address audience saturation (frequency 7.14). The correction was applied the same day Ciaran flagged it.

✓

BOF Retargeting Audience Expanded to 60 Days (June 9)

Frequency hit 9.17 on the 30-day audience of 2,295 people after only 5 days — well above the healthy 3–5x range. A new Web Visits Last 60 Days custom audience (ID: 120245054721650643) was created and swapped into the BOF ad set on June 9. By June 11 frequency had dropped to 2.51. Issue fully resolved. No budget change was required.

✓

Gas and Heating Oil Budget Approved by Ciaran (June 9)

Ciaran replied to the campaign brief submitted June 5: "Happy with the budget." Additional €60/day confirmed for two new campaigns: Gas Cylinders (€30/day) and Home Heating Oil (€30/day). Structures will be built June 16–20. Launch target July 1. Taking total account budget from €85/day to €145/day once both campaigns are live. Two blockers remain: heating oil landing page pricing (Graphedia contacted) and creative assets from Ciaran.

⚠

Compost: Second Creative Requested from Ciaran (June 11)

Three consecutive zero-purchase days (Jun 9–11) following 11 purchases in the previous 4 days triggered a review. The Compost Trees ad has been running as the sole active creative since June 5 when the inferior ad was paused. €62.44 was spent with 0 purchases, 1 ATC, and 1 abandoned checkout (€242.25 in cart) across those 3 days. A second image or short video has been requested from Ciaran to give the algorithm alternative creative and restore purchase momentum. The underlying product demand is confirmed strong (11 purchases in 4 days, 95.9x historical ROAS).

⚠

Pixel Purchase Value Issue Flagged to Graphedia (June 11–12)

Meta Events Manager diagnostics confirmed that 23% of Purchase events are firing without a value field. The pixel is triggering the purchase event correctly but not passing the order amount on certain orders. This affects ROAS reporting accuracy and Meta's ability to optimise toward higher-value orders. Graphedia have been asked which plugin manages the Meta Pixel on kennyfuels.ie so the specific fix can be identified and applied.

What's Next
Upcoming Priorities

Performance-driven priorities for the coming weeks.

Awaiting Ciaran

Compost: Second Creative Asset

Three consecutive zero-purchase days (Jun 9–11) signal creative fatigue on the Compost Trees ad. A second image or short video is needed. Once received, it will be added to the Compost ad set. The algorithm will then have two options to rotate, reducing dependence on a single creative and restoring consistent purchase delivery.

23% of Purchase events are missing the value field. Graphedia have been asked which plugin manages the pixel on kennyfuels.ie. Once confirmed, the fix will be applied to ensure all purchase events pass the correct order value. This affects both ROAS reporting accuracy and Meta's ability to optimise toward higher-value conversions.

Awaiting Graphedia

Heating Oil Landing Page Pricing

No pricing is currently visible on kennyfuels.ie/home-heating-oil-wexford-carlow-wicklow/. This is a blocker for the Gas and Heating Oil campaign launch on July 1. Graphedia have been contacted. Pricing must be live before the campaign goes live, as ads sending traffic to a page with no pricing will not convert.

June 16–20

Gas and Heating Oil Campaign Build

Budget approved at €60/day (€30 per campaign). Two campaigns to be built: Gas Cylinders and Home Heating Oil. Both geo-targeted to Wexford, Wicklow, Dublin, and Carlow. Launch date July 1, subject to landing page pricing being live and creative assets from Ciaran.

Review Jun 19

Sand+Stones Creative Split Review

Both ads running at €25/day. Ad 1 Sand tonne bags vs Ad 2 Stones. Review after 2 full weeks of data from the geography correction (Jun 9 onwards). If one ad is significantly outperforming, the weaker ad will be paused and the stronger ad will receive the full budget.

Monitor Weekly

BOF Retargeting Frequency

Frequency resolved to 2.51 after the 60-day audience expansion. Continue monitoring weekly. If frequency climbs above 5.0 again, the next step is to add Facebook and Instagram engagers (last 180 days) to the audience mix, which would expand the pool further without requiring a budget change.
